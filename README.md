Acute Encephalitis Syndrome (AES), locally known as "Chamki Bukhar," has a high incidence rate in specific districts of Bihar, such as Muzaffarpur and Vaishali.This project aims to predict health risks based on environmental and physiological markers. This tool serves as an early identification system to aid in effective disease management
